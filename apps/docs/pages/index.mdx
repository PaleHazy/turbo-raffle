#Turbo-Raffle
 
Welcome to the Turbo-Raffle project!

## What is Turbo-Raffle?

Turbo Raffle is at its core a drop shipping raffle application.
Turbo raffle seeks to encorporate with existing market place API's to allow users to create and participate in raffles for products.

As a starting market we have selected https://www.tcgplayer.com/ and will be interfacing with Magic, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Pokemon, Digimon, Flesh and Blood,
